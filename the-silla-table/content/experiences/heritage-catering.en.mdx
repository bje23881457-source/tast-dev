For twenty years we have catered events large and small — from intimate cultural dinners to international programmes for hundreds of guests. This is the service built for organisers.

## Made for MICE and groups

If you are planning a conference, a delegation visit, a wedding or a cultural programme, we design and deliver a menu rooted in Silla food heritage, scaled to your numbers and venue. The food arrives with its story, which makes it a programme element, not just a meal.

## What we handle

- **Menu design** around season, dietary needs and your budget.
- **On-site service** with our own team.
- **Cultural framing** — optional short storytelling so guests understand what they are eating.

## Good to know

- **Group size:** 20 to 300+; we have delivered at both ends.
- **Lead time:** The earlier the better, especially in peak season.
- **Meal included:** Yes — this *is* the catering.

Tell us your event date, headcount and venue, and we will prepare a proposal.
