Gyeongju is easier to reach than most visitors expect. Here is what actually works.

## From Seoul

Take the **KTX high-speed train** to *Singyeongju Station*. It runs in roughly two hours from Seoul Station. Book a day or two ahead in peak season. From Singyeongju, the old town is a 15-minute taxi or a local bus ride.

## From Busan

Busan is close — about 30 minutes by KTX to Singyeongju, or a scenic intercity bus. Gyeongju makes an easy day trip from Busan, though it rewards an overnight stay.

## Getting around town

- **On foot & by bike:** The historic core around Wolji and the royal tombs is flat and walkable; bike rentals are common.
- **City buses** cover the outlying sites like Bulguksa and Seokguram.
- **Taxis** are affordable for the temple sites up Tohamsan.

## A local tip

Base yourself near the old town, not the station. Everything worth seeing at night — and the best evening light — is in the historic centre.
