This is the experience the whole brand is named for. A table, set personally, where the food carries the story.

## The menu is a season

There is no fixed card. We cook what Gyeongju is growing and curing right now, arranged as a series of small courses. Each one comes with the two-minute story of why a Silla kitchen would recognise it — the fermented depth, the East Sea catch, the honeyed grain sweet to close.

## Hosted, not served

I sit with the table. Between courses we talk — about the dishes, about the city, about whatever the evening becomes. This is dinner as hospitality, not as transaction.
